Directions

  • Use the ↑, ↓, → and ← keys to shift tiles
  • The tiles, when shifted onto, will add up
  • The number 2 will appear on a random tile after every move
  • You win as soon as 2024 is gotten
  • You will lose as soon as the board is full and no tiles can be added
